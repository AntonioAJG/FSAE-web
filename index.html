About Us

We are FSAE TEC RACING, a passionate team of engineers and innovators building the future of motorsport. Our work spans across multiple subsystems including:

  • Aerodynamics – designing wings and bodywork to cut through the air with precision
  • Chassis – crafting a lightweight and rigid structure
  • Powertrain – developing high-performance propulsion systems
  • Electric – managing the energy that drives our machine
  • Suspension – optimizing grip, balance, and handling

But behind every great car, there’s a team of strategists making it possible. Our Administration Team leads the charge in:

  • Planning – structuring every stage of development
  • Marketing – showing the world who we are
  • Cost Analysis – keeping our performance on budget
  • Safety – ensuring every step is secure and compliant
  • Project Management – turning ideas into real, working cars

Together, we are more than just a student team — we are a racing family building engineering legends.
