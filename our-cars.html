Our Cars

At FSAE TEC Racing, our car is the product of countless hours of engineering, testing, and teamwork. Each subsystem is developed by specialists who push innovation to the limit.

Chassis: Our chassis is built from lightweight materials like aluminum and carbon fiber to ensure strength and performance.
Aerodynamics: We design custom wings, diffusers, and body panels to generate downforce and reduce drag.
Suspension: Tuned for performance and adjustability to suit different tracks.
Powertrain: Whether combustion or electric, our drivetrain is optimized for acceleration, efficiency, and control.
Electronics: We integrate sensors and custom ECUs for precision data logging and control systems.

We take pride in designing, simulating, manufacturing, assembling, and testing every component ourselves.
